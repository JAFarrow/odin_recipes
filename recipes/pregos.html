Prego Rolls

Prego Rolls, or prego no p√£o in the original Portuguese, is a simple workmans' sandwhich consisiting of thinly sliced beef tossed in a garlicky marinade and served on a crispy prego roll.

Despite the simplicity of the dish, it is incredibly delicious and serves as an excellent lunch option during warm summer days, especailly when served with crisp lettuce, carmalized onion and a cold beer.

Ingredients

  • Half Dozen Prego Rolls
  • 500g Thinly Sliced Minute Steaks
  • 1/2 Onion, sliced
  • 125ml Red Wine
  • 2 Cloves Garlic, crushed
  • 1 Red Chilli, chopped
  • 2 Medium Sized Tomatoes, roughly chopped
  • 50g Fresh Lettuce, shredded
