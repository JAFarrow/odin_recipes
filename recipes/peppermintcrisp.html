Peppermint Crisp Tart

Always to be found at any significent gathering of family and friends, peppermint crisp tart is a simple and delicious dessert sure to be enjoyed by all.

With no baking neccessary and little in the way of preparation needed, this fridge tart is almost a cop-out of a recipe, luckily a delicious one at that.

Ingredients

  • Ten Packets of Tennis Biscuits
  • Two Cans of Caramelized Condense Milk
  • Two Cups of Fresh Cream
  • Two Grated Peppermint Crisp Slabs

Steps

  1. Place a layer of whole tennis biscuits in a 29x19x5cm dish, take a smaller dish if you would like to do more layers.
  2. Smear the biscuits with one tin of caramel condensed milk, or if you are goign with more layers, use as needed.
  3. Beat the cream until almost stiff. Add the grated Peppermint Crisp, leaving enough to sprinkle on the top of the tart.
  4. Add a thin layer of the beaten cream with Peppermint Crisp on the layer of tennis biscuits, then arrange another layer of tennis biscuit on top. Repeat as needed.
  5. In the cream mixture, add one tin of caramel condensed milk, continue to mix. When done, finish off the tart by adding this cream mixture on top of the biscuits, sprinkle with the leftover Peppermint Crisp.
  6. Refrigerate for at least four to five hours, although at least six is neccesary for the best consistancy.
  7. Serve chilled and enjoy!
