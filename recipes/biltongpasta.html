Biltong Pasta

A rich and salty summer snack or starter, biltong pasta is a South African favourite.

Made with light and fresh ingredients, it is an excellent tastebud tantalizer to satisfy hungry braai guests before the main meal is served.

Ingredients

  • Two Cups of Fusilli Pasta
  • One Third Cup of Chopped Bell Peppers (Yellow Preferably)
  • A Chopped Red Onion Quarter
  • Half A Cup of Cubed Chedder Cheese
  • Half A Cup of Thinly Sliced Beef Biltong
  • One Third Cup of Mayonnaise
  • Quarter Cup of Sweet Chilli Sauce
  • Tablespoon of Mustard
  • Salt to Taste
  • Chopped Fresh Chives

Steps

  1. Cook the pasta according to package instructions and allow to cool.
  2. Once cool, mix the cooked pasta with all the other ingredients. Season to taste.
  3. Garnish with fresh chives and enjoy!
